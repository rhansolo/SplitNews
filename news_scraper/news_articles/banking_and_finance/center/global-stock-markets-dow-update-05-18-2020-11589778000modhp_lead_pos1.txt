The Dow Jones Industrial Average surged more than 900 points Monday, its best day since April 6, on hopeful developments surrounding a potential coronavirus vaccine.

A rebound in oil prices, a pledge of further stimulus from the head of the Federal Reserve and a gradual easing of lockdown measures also boosted sentiment. Of the 30 stocks in the blue-chip index, 29 rallied as did all 11 sectors of the S&P 500.
