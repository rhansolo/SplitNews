Playing board games and cards has always been a staple pastime for friends and family. But as we wait out the coronavirus pandemic, not everyone has access to their favorite Scrabble nemesis or poker cohorts.

Fortunately, the same internet that brought you reruns of “Seinfeld” to binge-watch also offers a range of classic games that can be played with far-flung opponents. No pricey console or fancy computer is required. All you need is a mobile device or a web browser, and many options are free or sold at a discount in bundles.
