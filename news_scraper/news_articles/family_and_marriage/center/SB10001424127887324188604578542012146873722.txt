A federal judge agreed to a government proposal that would make the Plan B emergency-contraceptive pill available in drugstores without age or other restrictions.

On Monday the Justice Department proposed a plan that would require Plan B manufacturers to seek approval from the Food and Drug Administration to sell the pill without a prescription to anyone regardless of age.
