LONDON—U.K. Prime Minister Theresa May on Sunday revealed the secret advice she received from President Donald Trump on how to handle Brexit talks: He suggested the U.K. sue the European Union.

At a joint press conference with the U.K. leader Friday, Mr. Trump referred to a “tough” and “brutal” negotiating ploy he had suggested Mrs. May try in her discussions with Brussels over the terms of Britain’s planned exit from the EU.
